Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our mobile application, website, or other services (collectively, the "Services").

We collect the following types of personal information from you:

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Choices
