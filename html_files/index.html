How CarboCredit Works

CarboCredit allows individuals and businesses to trade carbon credits securely on the XRPL blockchain. Here's a simple guide on how it works:

  1. Create an account on CarboCredit.org.
  2. Explore carbon credit listings and choose credits based on your sustainability goals.
  3. Initiate a secure transaction using XRPL blockchain technology.
  4. Monitor and verify your carbon credit transactions transparently on the blockchain.
  5. Contribute to a greener planet by supporting sustainable initiatives.
